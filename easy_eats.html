Easy Eats App


As someone who loves experimenting in the kitchen, I've always faced that frustrating moment when ingredients go to waste in my fridge. That's why I created this app! It lets you quickly log what's in your fridge and suggests recipes you can make with what you already have. You can filter recipes based on your preferences and save your favorites to build your own personalized cookbook over time.


I've built the core features of the app, and I'm now expanding it to include meal planning capabilities and advanced filters for dietary preferences.
